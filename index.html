Welcome!

I am Kevin Delgado, currently a sophomore at Duke University double majoring in Electrical & Computer Engineering and Computer Science. Growing up on Florida's Space Coast I consider myself incredibly fortunate to have been surrounded by and mentored by the incredible engineers that have made human spaceflight a reality. This has led to a lifelong passion for pushing the limits of engineering. In high school this resulted in me becoming an active member of my robotics team, FRC Team 233 "The Pink Team". Now in college, this passion continues as I have taken a great interest in solving problems through software.

Outside of my studies, I am proud to call myself a Cameron Crazie and a fan of all that is related to Duke Athletics. Additionally, since founding the Cocoa Beach High School Ultimate Club in tenth grade I have become an avid Ultimate Frisbee player, currently playing for Duke Brimstone.

Right now, I am looking for internship opportunites in the field of software development for the Summer of 2015. Feel free to check out some of my projects and contact me if you think I would be a good fit.
